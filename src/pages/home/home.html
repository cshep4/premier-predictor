Scoring Rules

WC 2018 Predictor

Rules:

Predictions for each match can be added/updated any time before the match kicks off. As the tournament progresses, the knockout matches will be populated with the correct teams, these predictions can also be made at any time up until each match kicks off. Predictions can be changed as often as you wish. Once a match kicks off, the prediction for that game will be locked in and cannot be changed.

WC 2018 Predictor

Scoring:

Group Stage 1 Pt. Correct amount of goals for a team. (e.g. prediction of 2-1, final score is 2-3, 1 point will be awarded. Prediction of 3-1, final score is 2-2, 0 points will be awarded.) 3 Pts. Correct result, plus points for correct amount of goals (if any). (e.g. prediction of 2-1, final score is 3-2, 3 points will be awarded for predicting the correct team to win.) 3 Pts. Correct scoreline, plus points for a correct result and goals. (e.g. prediction of 2-1, final score is 2-1, 8 points will be awarded - 3 for correct scoreline, 3 for correct result, 1 for each team's correct amount of goals) 5 Pts. Correct team position in a group. Knockout Stage 1 Pt. Correct amount of goals for a team, see above. 3 Pts. Correct result, plus points for correct amount of goals (if any), see above. 3 Pts. Correct scoreline, plus points for a correct result and goals, see above. 20 Pts. Correct prediction of the winner.
